About Phishing Detection Tool

This project aims to help users identify potentially dangerous phishing URLs quickly and effectively. Phishing attacks trick users into revealing sensitive information by masquerading as legitimate websites. Our tool provides multiple detection methods to increase your online safety.

Key Features

  • Dummy Keyword Detection: Simple and fast keyword matching to flag suspicious URLs.
  • Google Safe Browsing API: Integration with Google's trusted threat database for accurate results.
  • Clean, User-Friendly Interface: Easy to use and accessible from any device.
  • Dashboard: View recent URL checks and their safety status in one place.

Why It Matters

Phishing attacks are one of the most common cyber threats today, causing millions of dollars in damages annually. Being able to quickly check URLs helps users avoid falling victim to scams and protects their personal and financial data.
